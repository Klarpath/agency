Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

1. Introduction

KlarPath ("we," "our," or "us") operates the klarpath.co.uk website and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Name, email, phone number, company information
  • Billing and payment information (processed by secure payment providers)
  • Business data including analytics, performance metrics, customer information
  • Communications with our team via contact forms, email, or phone

Information Automatically Collected:

  • IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, referring website
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Location information (city/country level from IP address)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our digital marketing services
  • Send service updates, billing information, and customer support
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Analyze website usage and optimize user experience
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Create anonymized case studies and marketing materials

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we process your data based on:

  • Contract: Necessary to provide services you've requested
  • Consent: For marketing communications and tracking
  • Legitimate Interest: For analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory requirements

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics to understand user behavior
  • Marketing Cookies: To track conversions and optimize campaigns

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services including:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics
  • Google Ads: Conversion tracking
  • Payment Processors: Stripe, Square (secure payment processing)
  • Email Services: Mailchimp, SendGrid (email communications)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

7. Data Retention

  • Client data: Retained for duration of service + 3 years after termination
  • Analytics data: Retained for 26 months
  • Marketing cookies: Retained for 2 years
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

While we implement strong security, no method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR and UK CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in portable format
  • Objection: Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact: info@klarpath.co.uk

10. Marketing Communications

We send marketing emails only with your consent. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email or by contacting us directly.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us immediately.

12. International Data Transfers

If you're outside the UK/EU, data transferred to us may be subject to different laws. By using our services, you consent to such transfers. We use Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes take effect when posted. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Data Protection Officer

For privacy-related questions or complaints, contact: info@klarpath.co.uk

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

By using KlarPath services, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Privacy Policy.
